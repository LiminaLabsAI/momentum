Key signals:
- **`hooks: stable: true`** confirms ENH-037 — hooks default-on; momentum's `.codex/hooks.json` will fire without `[features] hooks = true` opt-in.

- **`enable_fanout: under development: false`** is the GATING SIGNAL for the `parallelSubagents` capability flip. Parallel-fanout (spawning multiple subagents concurrently) is NOT yet a stable Codex feature at codex-cli 0.133.0. Momentum's `parallelSubagents: false` for the Codex adapter stays correct.
- **`browser_use` + `computer_use: stable: true`** confirms the v0.20.1 capability flips were correct.
- **`skill_mcp_dependency_install: stable: true`** confirms Codex's skills surface is stable; momentum's `.agents/skills/<name>/SKILL.md` files are auto-discovered.
